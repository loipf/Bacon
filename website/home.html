Overview

gene expression group of the NEAP project

This website is part of the NEAP project consisting of many different analyses on atherosclerosis. The 3 developed tools have the goal of providing a unified platform for researchers that allows them to easily examine their data in terms of various aspects.

The main topic is gene expression and its analyses in patients with cardiovascular diseases. By uploading the gene expression count matrix of a patient/set of patients of interest we offer the possibility to get: 1) the set of significant differentially expressed genes as well as the disease state, 2) the enriched genes present in the data provided as well as a table stating the overiew on the diseases connected to those sigificant genes offering information regarding atherosclerosis specificity and 3) a set of corresponding mouse orthologs with the purpose of validating mouse models.

A more detailed view on the project and results from different steps can be found in the interactive workflow. A overview of all implemented studies and their content can be found in datasets. Paper we have used in our analysis are listed in sources.
